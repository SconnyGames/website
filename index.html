About Sconny Games

We make mobile games with simple rules, satisfying depth, and a strong “one more try” loop. Every build is tuned for feel, clarity, and smooth performance.

Crafted Feel

Animations, sound cues, and input response are tuned so the game feels “alive” from the first tap.

Built to Scale

We design for iteration: rapid prototyping, analytics-ready events, and clean content pipelines.

Player-First

We obsess over clarity: readable UI, fair difficulty curves, and meaningful progression.

What we do

From prototype to release, we focus on the parts that make mobile games succeed: retention, performance, and memorable moment-to-moment gameplay.

Game Design & Prototyping

Quick prototypes, tight core loops, and rapid iteration until the “fun” is undeniable.

Polish & Feedback

Juice, VFX, haptics, and audio layered carefully — satisfying without being noisy.

Launch & Live Ops

Analytics-friendly builds, experiments, and content updates designed to keep players engaged.
